## Common questions

<Accordion title="What is a headcanon?">
  A headcanon is a personal interpretation or belief about a character, storyline, or fictional universe that isn't explicitly stated in the original work. It's the "canon" that exists in your head. For example, you might have a headcanon about a character's childhood, their hidden talents, or their favorite food—details that enrich your understanding of the character even if they were never mentioned in the source material.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does the AI generator work?">
  The generator uses advanced AI language models trained on storytelling patterns and character development techniques. When you provide a character name and optional details, the AI analyzes your input and generates creative, contextually appropriate headcanons that feel authentic to the character. The AI considers narrative coherence, character consistency, and compelling storytelling to create headcanons that enhance your fan fiction writing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is the generated content free to use in my stories?">
  Yes, you're free to use the generated headcanons in your fan fiction stories, original works, or creative projects. The content generated by the tool is yours to use, adapt, and modify as you see fit. We encourage you to treat the generated headcanons as inspiration and starting points that you can refine and personalize for your specific writing needs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I generate headcanons for copyrighted characters?">
  Yes, you can generate headcanons for any character, including those from copyrighted works. Creating headcanons is a common practice in fan fiction communities. However, remember that if you're writing fan fiction featuring copyrighted characters, standard fan fiction guidelines apply—most fan works are created for non-commercial purposes and include appropriate disclaimers about character ownership.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I get better or more specific results?">
  To get more targeted headcanons, use the **Additional Details** field to provide context about what you're looking for. Here are some tips:
  
  - Mention the character's source material ("from Harry Potter," "from My Hero Academia")
  - Specify the type of headcanon you want ("childhood trauma," "hidden hobby," "relationship dynamics")
  - Include relevant personality traits or existing canon details
  - Describe the tone you're aiming for ("angsty," "wholesome," "humorous")
  
  The more specific your input, the more tailored the generated headcanon will be to your needs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What's the difference between the length options?">
  The length options determine how many headcanon points you'll receive:
  
  - **Very Short:** 1-2 headcanon points—perfect for quick inspiration
  - **Short:** 3-5 points—good for focused character aspects
  - **Medium:** 5-10 points—balanced detail for well-rounded characters
  - **Long:** 10-15 points—comprehensive character exploration
  - **Very Long:** 15+ points—extensive deep dive into character lore
  
  Choose shorter lengths when you need specific ideas, and longer lengths when you're building a character from scratch or want extensive background details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I save or export my generated headcanons?">
  You can easily copy the generated headcanons from the results page and paste them into your preferred writing tool, note-taking app, or document. We recommend saving headcanons you love immediately, as generating new content will replace the previous results on the page. Consider keeping a dedicated document or file for your character headcanons to reference while writing.
</Accordion>
