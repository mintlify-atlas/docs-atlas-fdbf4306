# Create your first headcanon

Get started with the Character Headcanon Generator and create compelling character backstories in just a few simple steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the generator">
    Go to [headcanongenerators.com](https://headcanongenerators.com/) to access the tool.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your character name">
    Fill in the **Character Name** field with the character you want to generate headcanons for.
    
    This can be an existing character from any fandom or an original character you've created.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select the headcanon length">
    Choose the **Length** that suits your needs:
    
    - **Very Short:** 1-2 headcanons
    - **Short:** 3-5 headcanons
    - **Medium:** 5-10 headcanons
    - **Long:** 10-15 headcanons
    - **Very Long:** 15+ headcanons
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add optional details (recommended)">
    Use the **Additional Details for Headcanon** field to provide context that will help the AI generate more relevant headcanons.
    
    <Tip>
      While this field is optional, adding details significantly improves the quality and relevance of your results.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate your headcanon">
    Click the **Generate** button and wait a few seconds for the AI to create your custom headcanon.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example generation

Here's what a typical generation might look like:

**Input:**
- **Character Name:** Luna Nightshade
- **Length:** Short (3-5)
- **Additional Details:** A detective in a cyberpunk setting who has a mysterious past and loves old jazz music

**What to expect:**
The generator will create 3-5 unique headcanons that incorporate your character's name and the details you provided, such as backstory elements, personality quirks, relationships, or habits that align with the cyberpunk detective theme and jazz music preference.

<Note>
  Each generation is unique. Running the same inputs multiple times will produce different creative results.
</Note>

## Tips for best results

Get the most out of the Character Headcanon Generator with these tips:

- **Be specific with additional details:** Include personality traits, setting information, relationships, or specific aspects of the character you want to explore
- **Choose the right length:** Start with Short or Medium to get a feel for the generator, then experiment with longer outputs
- **Provide context:** Mention the universe or genre your character belongs to for more authentic results
- **Iterate and refine:** Don't hesitate to generate multiple times with adjusted details to find headcanons that resonate
