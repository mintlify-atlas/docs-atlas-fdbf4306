The Length setting controls how many headcanons the AI generates for your character. This feature lets you choose between quick inspiration and comprehensive character exploration.

## The five length options

When you use the [Headcanon Generator](https://headcanongenerators.com/), select from these length options:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Very Short">
    **1-2 headcanons**
    
    Perfect for quick character insights or when you need a single compelling idea to spark your creativity.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Short">
    **3-5 headcanons**
    
    A focused set of related ideas that explore different aspects of your character without overwhelming detail.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Medium">
    **5-10 headcanons**
    
    The balanced option that provides substantial character depth while remaining digestible and actionable.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Long">
    **10-15 headcanons**
    
    Extensive character exploration covering personality, backstory, relationships, quirks, and motivations.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Very Long">
    **15+ headcanons**
    
    Comprehensive character lore that leaves no stone unturned, ideal for major characters or deep worldbuilding.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## When to use each length option

Choose your length based on your creative needs:

### Very Short (1-2 headcanons)

- Quick brainstorming sessions
- Minor or background characters
- When you need a single strong idea
- Testing different character concepts rapidly

<Tip>
Use Very Short when you're exploring multiple character variations and want fast results.
</Tip>

### Short (3-5 headcanons)

- Supporting characters in your story
- Focused character development in specific areas
- Adding depth to side characters
- Quick reference for roleplay scenarios

### Medium (5-10 headcanons)

- Main supporting characters
- Well-rounded character profiles
- Balanced exploration of personality and backstory
- Most versatile option for general use

<Note>
Medium length is the most popular choice, offering comprehensive insights without excessive detail.
</Note>

### Long (10-15 headcanons)

- Protagonist or main characters
- Complex character arcs
- Characters with rich backstories
- Detailed roleplay character sheets

### Very Long (15+ headcanons)

- Primary protagonists or antagonists
- Characters central to your narrative
- Comprehensive character bibles
- Deep worldbuilding projects
- Long-form fan fiction planning

<Info>
Longer outputs take slightly more time to generate but provide exponentially more creative material.
</Info>

## How length affects the depth of headcanons

Length impacts both quantity and the breadth of topics covered:

**Shorter lengths** (Very Short, Short) focus on:
- Core personality traits
- Standout quirks or habits
- Key defining characteristics
- Singular memorable aspects

**Medium length** covers:
- Personality traits and quirks
- Backstory elements
- Relationship dynamics
- Motivations and fears

**Longer lengths** (Long, Very Long) explore:
- Comprehensive personality profiles
- Detailed backstory and history
- Multiple relationship dynamics
- Hidden depths and contradictions
- Specific habits, preferences, and mannerisms
- Character growth potential
- Symbolic or thematic elements

## Examples of output differences between lengths

Here's how the same character might be treated across different lengths:

<Accordion title="Very Short example: 'Luna Starweaver' (fantasy wizard)">
  **Headcanon 1:** Luna collects moon water during every full moon and believes each lunar phase imbues the water with different magical properties.
  
  **Headcanon 2:** She hums constellation songs while casting spells, a habit she learned from her grandmother.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Short example: 'Luna Starweaver' (fantasy wizard)">
  The Very Short headcanons plus:
  
  **Headcanon 3:** Luna's familiar is a silver fox that can turn invisible during the day but glows softly at night.
  
  **Headcanon 4:** She keeps a detailed journal of her dreams, convinced they hold prophetic visions about cosmic events.
  
  **Headcanon 5:** Luna refuses to wear shoes indoors because she believes direct contact with the ground keeps her magically grounded.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Medium example: 'Luna Starweaver' (fantasy wizard)">
  The Short headcanons plus:
  
  **Headcanons 6-10:** Would include backstory elements (childhood training, magical accidents), relationship quirks (how she interacts with other wizards), deeper personality traits (her philosophical views on magic), specific spell preferences, and emotional vulnerabilities.
</Accordion>

<Tip>
Start with Medium length for new characters. You can always generate again with a different length if you need more or less detail.
</Tip>
