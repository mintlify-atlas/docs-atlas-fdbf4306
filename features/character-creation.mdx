The Character Name field is the foundation of your headcanon generation. This is where you tell the AI which character you want to explore, whether it's an established character from your favorite series or an original creation.

## How to input character names

Enter the character's name in the Character Name field on the [Headcanon Generator](https://headcanongenerators.com/) interface. You can use:

- Full names (e.g., "Hermione Granger")
- First names only (e.g., "Hermione")
- Nicknames or aliases (e.g., "The Doctor")
- Original character names (e.g., "Aria Moonstone")

<Note>
The AI processes character names to understand context from popular fictional universes. If your character shares a name with a well-known character, the AI may draw from that context unless you specify otherwise in Additional Details.
</Note>

## Existing characters vs original characters

The Headcanon Generator works with both types of characters, but the AI interprets them differently:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Existing characters">
    When you enter a character from an established fictional universe, the AI recognizes the context and generates headcanons that feel authentic to that world.
    
    **Examples:**
    - Characters from books, movies, TV shows, anime, or games
    - Well-known fictional personalities
    - Characters from popular fandoms
    
    The AI understands the character's universe and creates lore that complements existing canon.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Original characters">
    For original characters or lesser-known names, the AI takes a creative approach based on the name itself and any additional details you provide.
    
    **Best practice:** Use the Additional Details field to give the AI context about your original character's world, personality, or role.
    
    **Example:** If you enter "Kael Shadowmend" with details like "fantasy rogue in a steampunk world," the AI generates headcanons that fit that specific setting.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How the AI interprets character context

The AI-powered system analyzes your character input to understand:

1. **Fictional universe recognition** - Identifies if the character belongs to a known franchise or series
2. **Character archetype** - Infers personality traits and roles based on name patterns and universe context
3. **Thematic elements** - Detects genre conventions (fantasy, sci-fi, contemporary, etc.)
4. **Narrative potential** - Finds opportunities for compelling backstory and character depth

<Tip>
If the AI's interpretation doesn't match your vision, use the Additional Details field to guide the generation in the right direction.
</Tip>

## Working with characters from different fictional universes

The Headcanon Generator supports characters from any fictional universe:

<Accordion title="Popular fandoms">
  The AI has broad knowledge of mainstream fandoms including:
  - Books (Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, etc.)
  - Movies and TV (Marvel, Star Wars, etc.)
  - Anime and manga
  - Video games
  - Web series and podcasts
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Niche or lesser-known universes">
  For characters from smaller fandoms or indie works, provide context in the Additional Details field:
  - Mention the source material
  - Describe the universe's tone or setting
  - Include key character relationships
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Crossover characters">
  You can request headcanons that blend universes by specifying this in Additional Details:
  - "Hermione Granger in the Star Trek universe"
  - "What if this character existed in a modern AU"
</Accordion>

<Info>
The more specific you are with character context, the more tailored and authentic your headcanons will be.
</Info>
