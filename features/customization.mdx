The Additional Details field is your most powerful tool for customizing headcanon generation. While optional, this field allows you to direct the AI toward specific aspects of your character's personality, relationships, setting, or themes.

## How to use the Additional Details field

Access the Additional Details field on the [Headcanon Generator](https://headcanongenerators.com/) below the Length selector. Enter any information that helps the AI understand your creative vision:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Leave it blank">
    When you leave Additional Details empty, the AI generates headcanons based purely on the character name and any recognizable universe context.
    
    **Best for:**
    - Well-known characters from established fandoms
    - Exploring unexpected directions
    - Maximum creative freedom
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Add specific guidance">
    Provide details to shape the generation toward your specific needs.
    
    **Best for:**
    - Original characters
    - Specific themes or tones
    - Targeted character aspects
    - Alternative universe interpretations
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
You don't need to write complete sentences. Keywords and short phrases work perfectly for guiding the AI.
</Tip>

## Types of details that work well

The AI responds effectively to different categories of additional details:

### Personality traits

Describe your character's temperament, values, or mental state:

- "anxious but determined"
- "sarcastic with a hidden soft side"
- "obsessively organized"
- "charismatic leader with trust issues"

<Note>
Personality details help the AI generate headcanons about habits, reactions, and interpersonal dynamics.
</Note>

### Relationships

Specify key relationships or social dynamics:

- "estranged from their family"
- "best friends with [character name]"
- "rivals turned allies"
- "secretly in love with their mentor"
- "protective older sibling"

### Settings and universe context

Provide worldbuilding details or setting information:

- "modern AU coffee shop setting"
- "post-apocalyptic survivor"
- "steampunk inventor in Victorian London"
- "small-town supernatural detective"
- "space station engineer"

<Info>
Setting details are especially important for original characters or alternative universe interpretations of existing characters.
</Info>

### Themes and tone

Guide the emotional or thematic direction:

- "focus on found family themes"
- "gothic horror vibes"
- "hopeful and optimistic"
- "dealing with loss and grief"
- "slice-of-life everyday moments"

### Specific aspects to explore

Direct the AI toward particular character elements:

- "childhood trauma and healing"
- "unusual hobbies and interests"
- "hidden talents"
- "daily routines and rituals"
- "relationship with magic/technology"

## How additional details influence generation

The AI uses your additional details to:

1. **Filter relevant headcanons** - Prioritizes ideas that align with your specifications
2. **Set thematic tone** - Adjusts the emotional quality and atmosphere of headcanons
3. **Establish context** - Grounds headcanons in the specific universe or setting you describe
4. **Target specific areas** - Focuses on character aspects you want to develop
5. **Avoid mismatches** - Prevents headcanons that contradict your vision

<Accordion title="Example: Without additional details">
  **Character:** "Raven Blackwood"
  
  **Additional Details:** (empty)
  
  **Result:** The AI generates general headcanons based on the name's dark, mysterious implications—possibly gothic themes, serious personality, connection to birds or darkness.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Example: With additional details">
  **Character:** "Raven Blackwood"
  
  **Additional Details:** "bubbly kindergarten teacher who loves bright colors, afraid of actual birds"
  
  **Result:** The AI generates headcanons that play against the name's dark connotations—ironic contrast between name and personality, why parents chose that name, coping mechanisms for bird phobia, cheerful habits that surprise people.
</Accordion>

## Best practices for detail specification

Maximize the effectiveness of your additional details:

### Be specific but concise

**Good:** "grief-stricken detective hunting their partner's killer"

**Too vague:** "detective with problems"

**Too detailed:** "A detective who has been working for fifteen years and lost their partner exactly three months ago in a shootout at the docks and is now struggling with insomnia and has started drinking too much coffee while investigating..."

<Tip>
Aim for 1-3 sentences or 5-10 descriptive keywords. The AI works best with focused guidance, not entire backstories.
</Tip>

### Combine different detail types

Mix personality, setting, and thematic details for best results:

- "optimistic android learning humanity in a cyberpunk world"
- "burned-out healer, fantasy setting, focus on self-care and boundaries"
- "teenage shapeshifter, identity struggles, found family"

### Use keywords for targeted results

Single keywords can powerfully direct generation:

- "bookish"
- "rebellious"
- "traumatized"
- "comedic"
- "romantic"

### Specify what you want to avoid

You can also guide the AI by stating what you don't want:

- "not a chosen one, just a regular person"
- "no tragic backstory"
- "avoid romance-focused headcanons"

<Note>
Negative specifications work best when paired with positive guidance about what you do want.
</Note>

### Experiment with different approaches

Try multiple generations with varying details:

1. Generate once with minimal details
2. Generate again with specific personality traits
3. Generate a third time focusing on relationships or setting
4. Compare results and mix your favorite headcanons

<Info>
Each generation is unique. Running the tool multiple times with the same inputs produces different creative results.
</Info>
