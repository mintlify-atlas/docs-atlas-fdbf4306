## Community resources

Connect with other fan fiction writers and get inspired by the community.

<Info>
  **Join the conversation**
  
  Share your generated headcanons, get writing tips, and connect with fellow fan fiction authors in online communities:
  
  - r/FanFiction on Reddit—active community for all things fan fiction
  - Archive of Our Own (AO3)—popular platform for sharing fan works
  - FanFiction.net—one of the largest fan fiction archives
  - Writing Discord servers—real-time chat with other writers

## Response times

We aim to respond to all support requests within 24-48 hours during business days. Bug reports are prioritized and typically addressed within 24 hours. For urgent account issues, please mark your email subject line with "URGENT" for faster attention.

<Note>
  Before reaching out, check our [FAQ page](/faq) to see if your question has already been answered.
</Note>
